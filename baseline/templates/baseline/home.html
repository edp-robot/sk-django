Welcome to baseline

Deploy Django on Heroku in seconds.

What is baseline?

Basline is a ready-made Django project that helps you get working on what your application does rather than repetetive boilerplate. The project includes some best-of-breed selections to get you off the ground quickly. Heroku makes getting your application out to the world a single-command operation.

What is included?

Baseline includes a great mix of Django apps, JavaScripts, and CSS to get you ramped up quickly.
